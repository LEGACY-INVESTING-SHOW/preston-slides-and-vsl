How To Claim The Home Office Deduction

Five steps to document business use, protect the write-off, and hand your CPA everything needed for Form 8829.

01

Pick Your Space

Use a spare bedroom, office corner, or converted garage. Measure that workspace, then divide it by your total home square footage.

Most people land at 10% to 20%
02

Take Photos

Capture before-and-after proof that the space is set up for business: desk, computer, whiteboard, filing cabinet, and the rest of your work setup.

Shows regular + exclusive use
03

Keep A Simple Log

You do not need to track every hour. Just keep a lightweight record of which days you worked there and what you were doing.

Protection if questioned later
04

Track Real Expenses

Keep mortgage interest or rent, utilities, internet, property taxes, homeowners insurance, and repairs in one folder or spreadsheet.

These are the CPA inputs
05

File Form 8829

Your CPA handles the filing, but now you know the form, the documentation behind it, and exactly what numbers go into the deduction.
